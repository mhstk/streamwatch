# Privacy Policy for StreamWatch

**Last updated: January 15, 2026**

## Overview

StreamWatch is a browser extension that transforms video links into a Netflix-style streaming experience. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### When You Sign In (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used to identify your account
- **Display name** - Shown in the extension interface
- **Profile picture** - Displayed in the extension

### Watch History

When you use StreamWatch, we store:
- **Video URLs** - Links to videos you've watched
- **Watch progress** - Your playback position in each video
- **Series data** - Videos you've organized into series

## How We Use Your Data

- **Account sync** - Your watch history syncs across devices when signed in
- **Resume playback** - Remember where you left off in videos
- **Series management** - Keep track of episodes in a series

## Data Storage

- **Local storage** - Watch history is stored locally in your browser
- **Cloud sync** - If signed in, data syncs via Google Firebase (secured by Google's infrastructure)

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Track your browsing activity outside of StreamWatch
- Collect data when you're not using the extension

## Data Retention

- **Local data** - Remains until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension
- **Cloud data** - Remains until you delete your account or request deletion

## Your Rights

You can:
- Use StreamWatch without signing in (local-only mode)
- Sign out at any time to stop cloud sync
- Clear your watch history from the extension settings
- Request deletion of your data by contacting us

## Third-Party Services

StreamWatch uses:
- **Google Firebase** - For authentication and data sync
- **Google Sign-In** - For account authentication

These services have their own privacy policies:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

StreamWatch is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated date.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, please open an issue on our GitHub repository:
https://github.com/mhstk/streamwatch/issues
